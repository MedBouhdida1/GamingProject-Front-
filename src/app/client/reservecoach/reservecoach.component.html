Description

Hi, I'm Jaydon Curtis I'm a professional League of legends player who played for almost one year and a half in the Italian ERL Pg Nationals. I hit Grand master 400 lp in s10 and 300 lp in s11.

The main point of this coaching is to improve your quality of life in ranked games and to get as many lp as possible the fastest way. Of course i believe that coaching should be focussed on becoming a better player but first you need to be in a good elo where you can play in an healthy environment skills whise and macro whise.

I'm going to teach you how to think, how to find the best way to win the game.

  • Playing from behind/ahead
  • Build creation for your main champions (choosing the right items in the game/explaining)
  • Wave management
  • Micro / Macro decision making
  • Challenger Coach
  • All you ever need to improve
  • Super friendly and engaging
